Why?

The idea originated from the DataKind events held around the world. Captain Alvin, the event chief, attended one such event in the UK and was so taken by the idea that he wanted to replicate the concept here in Sydney.

The event is run over a weekend and a collective of skilled volunteers (data analysts, scientists, engineers and designers) will work in teams to solve a key problem for a not-for-profit.
