20 - 21st February, 2016

Our very first Goodwill Analytics event happened on the weekend of the 20 - 21st of February, 2016. With just 25 places for Data Volunteers, there was a great deal of buzz around the event and the response was overwhelming - we were oversubscribed within a week.

The NFPs that participated in the event were Hello Sunday Morning, Refugee Council of Australia and The Shepherd Centre.

48 hours, countless slices of pizza and a few arcade games later, the event was over and it was a great success. 83% of data volunteers surveyed expressed that they learned something new from the event, while 100% said that they would participate in another Goodwill Analytics event again.
