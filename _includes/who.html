The Who

Not For Profit Organisations (NFPs)

The volunteers may have the superpowers, but the NFPs and the causes they support are the heart of our endeavours.

NFPs dedicate all of their time and profits to aiding their causes - from supporting underprivileged individuals and communities, to aiding research to find cures for life-threatening illnesses, to fighting for animal rights.

We’re all familiar with the causes, but often we don’t quite know how to help.

Prior to the Goodwill Analytics event, the NFPs will identify a number of key problems. The biggest ticket problems, with relevant supporting data, will be selected to be tackled at the event.

If you are a Not-For-Profit Organisation and are interested in participating in a Goodwill Analytics event, please [reach out to us].

Data Ambassadors

The leaders at the forefront of the event.

Our Data Ambassadors are data professionals just like the rest of the volunteers, but they have the chance to collaborate more closely with their NFP.

The Ambassadors will meet up with their NFP prior to the event to gather data and work with them to identify those big-ticket problems.

Before the event, they will also have done some preparation for the way their NFP’s problems could be solved and will guide their team of data volunteers in the right direction, helping to drive optimal outcomes and collect results.

If you are interested in being at Data Ambassador at a Goodwill Analytics event, please [reach out to us].

Volunteer Data Professionals

The superheroes of the event.

The number crunchers extraordinaire. The pattern spotters. The abstract and out-of-box thinkers. The ones with the superpowers for crying out loud!

After hearing about the causes each NFP supports and learning about the problems they face, you will choose which cause you would like to work on for the weekend.

You will form small teams and, after a brief exchange of pleasantries and perhaps a humble brag, you’ll knuckle down and lend your skills for a worthy cause!
