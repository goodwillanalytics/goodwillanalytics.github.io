What?

This is a weekend event to connect data professionals with the good causes championed by not-for-profit organisations.

Each not-for-profit organisation will present an issue they are currently facing and the volunteer data enthusiasts spend the weekend providing insights and solutions ideas found in the data of the organisation and any other piece of publicly available information.

These are real-world problems, issues the organisations are grappling with currently. The insights and solutions provided will shape a more positive direction for both the not-for-profit organisations and the causes they support.
