What is it?

This ia a weekend event to connect data professionals and the good causes championed by not-for-profit orgnaisations. Each not-for-profit organisation presents an issue they are grappling with currently and the volunteer data enthusiasts spend the weekend providing insights and solutions found in the data of the not-for-profit organisation.

These are real-world problems, issues the organisation is grappling with currently. The insights and solutions provided will shape a more positive direction for both the not-for-profit organisations and the causes they support.
