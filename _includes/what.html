What?

Goodwill Analytics is a weekend event seeking to connect volunteer data professionals with not-for-profit organisations for the purpose of applying the skills of the volunteers to help solve a problem the NFP may be facing.

Each not-for-profit organisation will be championed by a Data Ambassador who will essentially be the team lead for the volunteers. The volunteer data enthusiasts teams will spend the weekend providing insights and solutions ideas found in the data of the organisation and any other piece of publicly available information.

These are real-world problems, issues the organisations are grappling with currently. The insights and solutions provided will shape a more positive direction for both the not-for-profit organisations and the causes they support.
